Biography:


Yifan Wei (Cindy) is currently a PhD student at Beihang University with interests in robotics. She has earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Master of Engineering degree in System Engineering from Cornell University. She has outstanding academic performance, making to the Dean’s List a few times as both an undergrad and a graduate student. Cindy’s current Ph.D research focuses on haptics technology and robotics. This work has garnered interest for its innovative approach to combining new artificial intelligence technologies with the field of robotics.
