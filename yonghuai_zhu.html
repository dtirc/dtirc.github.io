Biography:


I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Beijing University of Technology. My academic journey includes obtaining a bachelor's degree from Luoyang Institute of Science and Technology and a master's degree from Jimei University. During my Ph.D., I am a member of the "Service-oriented Intelligent Manufacturing" Young Scientist Team of Beihang University and a member of the Beihang makeTwin team. Under the supervision of Dr. Fei Tao (Beihang University) and Dr. Zhifeng Liu (Jilin University), my main research areas are digital twins, intelligent manufacturing, complex equipment operation and maintenance, and the method of data model fusion.
