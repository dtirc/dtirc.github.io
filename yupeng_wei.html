Biography:


Dr. Yupeng Wei is currently an associate professor at the Digital Twin International Research Center (DTIRC) of Beihang University. Prior to joining Beihang University, he was an assistant professor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at San Jose State University, CA, USA. He received the B.S. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China, in 2014, the M.S. degree from the University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, USA, in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree from the Pennsylvania State University, State College, PA, USA, in 2020, all in Industrial Engineering. His current research interests include hybrid model-based and data-driven prognostics and health management, and artificial intelligence-enabled smart manufacturing. He has led/participated in several research projects supported by the Digital Manufacturing and Cybersecurity Institute and the United States Department of Transportation. He is a co-author of over 20 publications, on international journals and conference proceedings.
