Biography:


Yubo ZHOU completed his PhD at the School of Materials Science and Engineering at Beihang University and is currently serving as a postdoctoral researcher at the same university. During his graduate study, Yubo ZHOU conducted research on simulation technology for the autoclave process, liquid molding process, and pressure molding process for advanced composite materials. His current research interests mainly cover the applications of machine learning in materials sciences, intelligent manufacturing of advanced composite materials, and full-life cycle management of composites based on digital twins.
