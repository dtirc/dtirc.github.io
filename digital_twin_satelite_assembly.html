Digital twin satellite engineering: concept, framework and application


The concept of digital twin satellite engineering (DTSE) was proposed, the overall framework of DTSE was also illustrated from time dimension and space dimension. The key technologies of DTSE were put forward. The application of DTSE in different scenarios were also discussed from life cycle dimension, including satellite constellation design, detailed design, production, on-orbit service and health management, satellite network operation and maintenance.

Digital Twin Satellite Engineering


Nowadays, the development of large-scale satellite constellation such as SpaceX, OneWeb, is attracting worldwide attention. Requirements such as product diversification, short lead-time, mass production, have become challenges to satellite industry. To mitigate these challenges, we introduced digital twin into satellite engineering and specific satellite industrial scenarios, to realize satellite digital engineering and improve capability and efficiency of satellite industry.

Digital twin satellite assembly shop-floor: theory and application


The framework of digital twin satellite assembly shop-floor for mass production was proposed. To solve the systemic synchronization problem of digital twin satellite assembly shop-floor, a 5M synchronization mechanism was presented, and the specific methods were studied. Based on above research, a digital twin satellite assembly shop-floor system was developed. Meanwhile, a just-in-time material distribution method based on digital twin satellite assembly shop-floor was studied to solve the problem of resource competition and path conflict of logistics in mass satellite assembly shop-floor.

Digital twin satellite network: theory and application


The architecture of digital twin satellite network for large-scale satellite constellation was presented. To satisfy the requirements of large-scale satellite constellation design and capacity verification of communication, a satellite network simulation platform for digital twin satellite network was developed, which realized the rapid model construction of satellite constellation, the communication simulation for various protocols, and the evaluation of communication capability.
