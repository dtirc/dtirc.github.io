Biography:


He is currently serving as a visiting scholar at the DTIRC of Beihang University. After receiving his Ph.D from Beihang University, he continued his postdoctoral research at Beihang University with the "Excellence 100" Program, and at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University with the “Hong Kong Scholar” Program. His research interests span the areas of digital engineering, machine/deep learning, reliability design towards aeroengine/robot, and etc. He has led/participated in several research projects supported by the National Natural Science Foundations of China and the National Science and Technology Major Projects. As the first/corresponding author, he had published over 40 journal papers in his research fields.
