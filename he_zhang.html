Biography:


I am currently serving as a postdoctoral researcher at the DTIRC of Beihang University. My academic journey includes earning bachelor's and doctoral degrees from the Beihang Univerisity. My primary research revolves around the theory and technologies of digital twin modeling towards smart manufacturing and digital twin-based virtual testing towards aerospace. I have authored or co-authored more than 10 research papers in this field, accumulating over 5800 citations on Google Scholar. Additionally, I have been honored some awards and scholarships, including the first prize of China Machinery Industry Science and Technology in 2022, the 7th China Association for Science and Technology Excellent Scientific Paper Award, and the Capital Frontier Academic Achievement Award.
