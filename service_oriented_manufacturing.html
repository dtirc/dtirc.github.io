Manufacturing service combination recommendation


A novel multi-attribute recommendation method of manufacturing service combination considering the historical collaboration relationship of the online platforms is proposed. First, an evaluation indicator called manufacturing service collaboration frequency indicator (CFI) is proposed, which uses improved machine learning algorithm combining FP-growth and Simrank to mine the frequent terms and similarity of manufacturing service collaboration process. Then, a multi-objective evolutionary algorithm considering CFI and QoS is proposed to recommend a more reliable combination of manufacturing services for customers.

Service-oriented Manufacturing (SCOM)


With the continuous development of new IT technologies, such as distributed computing, artificial intelligence, Internet of Things, big data, etc., manufacturing resources are more easily perceived and shared from the physical world to the information world, which leads to the emergence and gradual maturity of the industrial Internet platforms. Manufacturing enterprises package their manufacturing resources into various manufacturing services and put them on the platforms. How to effectively use these manufacturing services is the focus of our research.

Platform-based Manufacturing Service Collaboration


The evolution of Manufacturing Service Collaboration (MSC) has progressed through three distinct phases: peer-to-peer collaboration, intra-enterprise collaboration, and socialized collaboration. Apart from peer-to-peer collaboration, platform-based collaboration has emerged as the predominant approach to manufacturing collaboration. The main distinction stems from the changes in operational decision-making driven by variations in platform-accessible resources and user numbers. This implies that as platforms accumulate more services and demands, operational decisions become increasingly complex. Consequently, a growing number of researchers are focusing their attention on addressing the challenges associated with platform-aggregated MSC operations. Through their efforts, they have contributed a wealth of valuable insights and findings to this burgeoning field of study.
