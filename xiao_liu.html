Biography:


I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in DTIRC at Beihang University, focusing on applying Digital Twin in healthcare area. I have a background of biology, which was my major during undergraduate study at Nankai University. I switched to the field of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering as a graduate student and started some computational work in bio-system at Johns Hopkins University. I ended up exploring computational biology in Biomedical Engineering at Purdue University. My multidisciplinary background allows me to work in the healthcare Digital Twin area at DTIRC in Beihang University.
