Research topics


The DTIRC is located at the forefront of the digital twin field and is dedicated to promoting the deep integration of digitalization and intelligent technologies in different fields, such as digital twin cities, digital twin aerospace equipment, digital twin smart manufacturing, digital twin energy, digital twin healthcare, and other related fields. The DTIRC has made outstanding achievements in the field of digital twins, such as five-dimensional model for digital twins and the makeTwin Software Reference Architecture. These scientific achievements mark the in-depth exploration and practical application of digital twin fields and provide innovative and practical solutions for the digital transformation of industries. The DTIRC will continue to promote the progress of digital twin technology and make greater contributions to the intelligent and sustainable development of industries in future.
