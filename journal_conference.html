Journals and conferences hosted or initialized by DTIRC


The DTIRC has established two international journals: "Digital Twin" and "Digital Engineering." The "Digital Twin" has been selected as a high-level new journal by the China Association for Science and Technology (CAST). These journals serve as important platforms for academic exchange both domestically and internationally, aiming to promote knowledge sharing and cutting-edge research in the fields of digital twins and digital engineering, providing an international forum for the academic community. Additionally, the DTIRC has initiated the Digital Twin International Conference, successfully hosting three years. This international conference focuses on the latest research advancements in digital twins, attracting scholars and professionals from around the world to facilitate international collaboration and exchange in the field. The platform offers an opportunity for in-depth discussions and experience sharing within the academic community. Moreover, the DTIRC has established the Digital Twin Global Forum, bringing together academic elites such as academicians, journal editors, and association fellows from various countries to share the latest research developments in digital twins. This not only promotes the collision and integration of academic ideas but also strengthens the connections within the international digital twin research community, providing valuable references for future research directions.

Digital Twin by Taylor & Francis


Publisher: Taylor & Francis

Chief Advisor: Fei Tao

Assistant Chief Advisor: Qinglin Qi


Digital Twin is a rapid multidisciplinary open access publishing platform for state-of-the-art basic, scientific and applied research on digital twin technologies. Articles are published using a fully transparent, author-driven model: the authors are solely responsible for the content of their article. Invited peer review takes place openly after publication, and the authors play a crucial role in ensuring that the article is peer-reviewed by independent experts in a timely manner.

Digital Engineering by Elsevier


Publisher: Elsevier

Editor-In-Chief: Fei Tao

Handling Editor: Li Yi


The Digital Engineering is an international peer reviewed journal that covers a broad range of topics in the frontier of digital engineering. The related fields including smart manufacturing, smart aerospace, automotive, ship, building, energy, healthcare, mining, architecture, smart city, etc. The journal communicates original contributions primarily in the form of research and review articles.

DigiTwin2023: The 3rd Digital Twin International Conference


From October 11th to 13th, 2023, the third International Conference on Digital Twin (DigiTwin 2023) was successfully held. With the theme "Making Digital Twins for a Changing World," DigiTwin 2023 aimed to bring together renowned scholars worldwide, share the latest research findings, explore technological frontiers, establish a high-level international platform for exchange, and lead innovation and development in the field of digital twins. The conference was held in a hybrid format, combining online and offline sessions. The event included workshops, on-site meetings, and an award ceremony, with over 2,100 experts and scholars from around the world registering to participate.

DigiTwin2022: The 2nd Digital Twin International Conference


From 26th-28th Sept.2022, under the strategic guidance of the Digital Twin International Advisory Committee(DTIAC), the 2nd Digital Twin International Conference (DigiTwin 2022) is successfully held. Oriented to the key and hot development direction in the field of the digital twin, DigiTwin 2022 aims to gather world-renowned scholars to share the latest scientific research results, discuss technological frontiers, build a high-level international exchange platform and promote the innovation and development of digital twin. DigiTwin 2022 is held in online mode via ZOOM and Tencent Meeting due to the impacts of the global pandemic, and more than 1900 scholars have registered and participated worldwide.

 DigiTwin2021: The 1st Digital Twin International Conference


The 1st Digital Twin International Conference jointly with the IMCC 2021 Digital Twin Forum were successfully held from November 18 to 19, 2021. The forum was organized by Prof. Fei Tao of the Beihang University entrusted by IMCC 2021. In view of the impact of the epidemic, the forum adopts an online format, and the Zoom and Tencent web conference rooms were held simultaneously. The Digital Twin International Conference on the afternoon of November 19th was hosted by Prof. Fei Tao and co-hosted by Prof. Ying Cheng from the Beihang University. In the conference, 6 experts and scholars from both national and international research institutions have given invited reports. After the reports, Prof. Fei Tao made a summary of the meeting.

The Digital Twin Global Forum (DTGF) consists of a series of regular online webinars delivered by members of the Digital Twin International Advisory Committee (DTIAC), authors of Digital Twin articles, and renowned experts. The DTGF is an international platform for exchanges and cooperation on digital twin, to jointly explore the innovative theories and methods of digital twin, promote the implementation of large-scale digital twin applications, and better respond to the challenges faced in digital twin research and practice.
