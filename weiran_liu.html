Biography:


Weiran Liu is currently a PhD student at Beihang University. He received his bachelor’s degree at School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering of Beihang University. He is currently working toward the PhD degree at the DTIRC. His research interests include digital twin theory and technology, digital engineering, theory and application technology of digital twin satellite and digital twin shop-floor system.
