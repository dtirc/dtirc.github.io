Biography:


Hongting Liu is currently a M.S. student at Beihang University. She has earned a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Automation from Beihang University. Her research interests include platform-aggregated manufacturing service collaboration and operation optimization and complex system-oriented digital twin shop-floor modeling construction and operation optimization.
