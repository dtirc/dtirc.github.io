Biography:


I am currently serving as an associate professor at Tianmushan Laboratory, Beihang University. My academic journey includes obtaining a Bachelor's degree from Zhengzhou University, a Master's degree from Taiyuan University of Technology, and a Doctoral degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I completed my doctoral studies in 2021 under the supervision of Professor Shiqi Li and continued my post-doctoral research until 2024 at the Center for Psychological Sciences, Zhejiang University. I joined Tianmushan Laboratory in April 2024. My research interests encompass human-robot interaction, robot imitation learning, AI for robotics, and robot control.
