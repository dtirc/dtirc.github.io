Biography:


Dr. Fei Tao is a distinguished professor at Beihang University, currently serving as a PI of DTIRC and the Executive Vice Dean of the International Research Institute for Multidisciplinary Science. His research focuses on digital twins, smart manufacturing, and green and sustainable manufacturing. After completing postdoctoral research at the University of Michigan, Dr. Tao returned to China in 2009 as an associate professor at Beihang University, earning promotion to full professor in 2013. With significant contributions, including five monographs and over 30 highly cited papers, as well as the first Nature paper on digital twins, Dr. Tao stands out in the global academic community. He has been named one of the top 20 most influential scholars in Smart Manufacturing by the Society of Manufacturing Engineers (SME) in 2021 and recognized as one of the Highly Cited Researchers by Clarivate from 2019 to 2023. Additionally, Dr. Tao founded the international journals "Digital Twin" and "Digital Engineering" and initiated the "Digital Twin International Forum" and "Digital Twin International Conferences." His outstanding contributions have earned him numerous national and international awards, further solidifying his esteemed position in the academic realm.
