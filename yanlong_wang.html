Biography:


I am a Ph.D. student at Beihang University. From 2017 to 2021, I obtained my bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering from Wuhan University of Technology. In the undergraduate study, I participated in a lot of competitions, such as the National College Student Mechanical Innovation Design Competition, etc., and won several awards. In 2021, I got the chance to pursue a doctorate degree at the School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering of Beihang University. My current research focuses on digital testing, real-time data transmission and error compensation between virtual environments and real equipment, digital engineering, digital manufacturing, etc.
