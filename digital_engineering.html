Digital engineering and its ten application outlooks


In this work, the theory of digital engineering was introduced, which strives to fully utilize both the ‘digital power’ and ‘intelligence power’ to enhance the ‘capability’. A five-phase maturity model of digital engineering was proposed. Then, the ‘digital power’, ‘intelligence power’ and ‘capability’ of digital engineering were defined, and the demands and challenges of digital engineering were expounded in detail. Furthermore, the architecture of digital engineering was designed. Finally, the application outlooks of digital engineering in ten fields were discussed.

Digital engineering


Digital engineering is a paradigm that strives to fully utilize both the ‘digital power’ and ‘intelligence power’ in such a way as to enhance the ‘capability’ of systematically solving problems and meeting requirements. Digital twin is an important means for empowering traditional industries and developing the digital economy. However, the digital engineering is still in the preliminary stage of development. Our research aims to continuously enrich the digital enginerring theoreries and applications and validate them in various industrial fields.
