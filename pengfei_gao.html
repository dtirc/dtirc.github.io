Biography:


I am a Ph.D. candidate at Beihang University's DTIRC, having completed my undergraduate degree in Information Engineering at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics and my master's degree in Control Engineering at Zhejiang University. During my master's studies, I delved into research on industrial internet interconnection technology and intelligent device control algorithms under the guidance of Dr. Wenjun Huang. Now, at DTIRC, I am dedicated to pursuing my Ph.D. with a focus on digital engineering, digital twin technologies, and digital testing of complex equipment, approaching these endeavors with a positive and energetic spirit.
