Biography:


I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at DTIRC and the School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering at Beihang University. Having earned both my bachelor's and master's degrees from Wuhan University of Technology, my master's studies delved into reconfigurable digital twin robotic manufacturing systems. In my ongoing Ph.D. research, I concentrate on digital twin technologies, digital engineering, and smart manufacturing, particularly emphasizing the construction and optimization of digital twin shop-floor operations. My enthusiasm lies in tackling and resolving problems, coupled with a keen interest in deciphering the underlying logic behind macro phenomena.
