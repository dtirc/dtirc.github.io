Biography:


I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees in Shandong Liaocheng and earned my doctoral degree in Shanghai. Following the completion of my Ph.D., I joined Beihang University in Beijing as a postdoctoral researcher, focusing on research related to digital twin equipment and makeTwin. During this period, I have published over 10 SCI/EI academic papers, including 2 highly cited papers in ESI. I have applied for more than 10 invention patents, participated in the drafting of 2 white papers on digital twins, led one project funded by the central university fund, and contributed to over 10 research projects, including the National Key Research and Development Program by the Ministry of Science and Technology, National Natural Science Foundation projects, and Ministry of Industry and Information Technology projects.
