Biography:


I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees in Inner Mongolia and eamed my doctoral degree in Beijing. Following the completion of my Ph.D., i joined Beihang University in Beijing as a postdoctoral researcher, focusing on research related to digital twin equipment and makeTwin. During this period, l have published over 10 SCl/El academic papers as first author. I have applied for more than 5 invention patents. I designed five non-standard mechanical equipment. I contributed to over 5 research projects, including national major project, provincial and ministerial projects.
