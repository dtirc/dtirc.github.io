Successful 3rd Digital Twin International Conference in Paris!

From October 11th to 13th, 2023, the third International Conference on Digital Twin (DigiTwin 2023) with the theme "Making Digital Twins for a Changing World" was successfully held in Paris. The conference was held in a hybrid format, combining online and offline sessions with over 2,100 participants around the world.

Next DigiTwin Conference!

Workshop of the Standard Development Committee

On the 8th December, the workshop for the Standard Development Committee on "Digital Twin Software Platform Reference Architecture" was successfully held by the DTIRC. Around 60 experts from 43 organizations have attended the workshop.

Winning Tencent Xplorer Prize!

Congratulations to Prof. Fei Tao for winning the Tencent Scientific Exploration Award in recognition of his outstanding contributions to advanced manufacturing!

The Digital Twin International Research Center (DTIRC) is one of the global leading organizations in digital twin research. The DTIRC has spearheaded significant research in digital twin theory and industrial applications, yielding innovative outcomes. Pioneering concepts like digital twin shopfloor and engines, the DTIRC has proposed digital twin networks and led a consortium of over ten universities in organizing the Digital Twin International Conference. Additionally, the DTIRC published the first Nature digital twin journal article, marking a milestone in advancing this transformative technology. The DTIRC runs two prestigious academic journals, further solidifying its role as a cornerstone in driving cutting-edge research and applications in the evolving field of digital twins.

About DTIRC

Director of DTIRC

Professor Dr. Fei Tao serves as the Director of DTIRC. His research focuses on digital twin, digital engineering, and smart manufacturing. He has authored 5 books and 100+ journal papers, including 30 ESI highly cited papers and the pioneering digital twin paper in Nature. He was selected as a highly cited scholar with over 40k+ citations and H-Index of 87. He was also recognized as "20 Most Influential Academics in Smart Manufacturing" by SME and honored with several prestigious national and international awards.
