Human Digital Twin: Data-driven modeling to predict future health condition


The human digital twin model aims at predicting future health condition of a person, inclusion the risk of critical disease (such as cancer, stroke and heart attack) from his/hers history health record as well as the health record of the whole population. The human DT model will be packed into program and platform, with the function of recording health record of individuals and population, predicting future health conditions with real-time input, as well as providing personalized medical and daily suggestions accordingly.

EBOV digital model


The application of Digital Twin in healthcare is still a new area, yet with a promising future. Common people would like to know their potential future health condition, doctors would like to learn the outcomes of their treatment in advance, and policy makers would like to acquire health profile of the population in the future. We would like to propose the framework for  digital twin healthcare and build the human digital twin in a combination of data-driven and mechanism-driven way. We hope our work can lead the development of Digital Twin healthcare.
