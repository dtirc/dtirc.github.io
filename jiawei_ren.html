Biography:


I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering at Beihang University under the supervision of Prof. Fei Tao. My academic journey includes obtaining a bachelor's degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017 and a master's degree in Automation from Sheffield University in 2018. Throughout my doctoral research, I aim to conduct comprehensive studies in platform-based manufacturing service collaboration and operation optimization, as well as digital twin-enhanced aero-engine fleet maintenance. I am enthusiastic about fostering collaborations and exchanging ideas with scholars, researchers, and industry professionals on both national and international levels, with the goal of collectively addressing industry challenges. If you share an interest in my research or have valuable insights, I welcome your collaboration and look forward to fruitful discussions with scholars and professionals.
