Biography:


I am presently pursuing a Ph.D. at the DTIRC of Beihang University. My academic journey began with a bachelor's and master's degree from Wuhan University of Technology. During my master's program, under the mentorship of Professor Lei Wang, I immersed myself in research focused on manufacturing enterprise collaboration utilizing the industrial Internet platform. Serving as a crucial team member in various collaborative projects with enterprises, I contributed to the development of tailored intelligent manufacturing solutions. Upon joining DTIRC, I proactively engaged in research under the guidance of professors, focusing on areas such as intelligent control of manufacturing processes, product quality prediction, and Prognostics and Health Management (PHM).
