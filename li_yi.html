Biography:


I am currently serving as an associate professor at the DTIRC of Beihang University. My academic journey includes earning a bachelor's degree from the University of Wuppertal and master's and doctoral degrees from the Technical University of Kaiserslautern. During my Ph.D., I worked as a research engineer at the Institute for Manufacturing Technology and Production Systems (FBK) and was a member of the German-US joint Graduate School "International Research Training Group (IRTG) 2057." Completing my doctoral studies in 2021 under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Jan C. Aurich and Prof. Dr. Bahram Ravani (UC Davis), I continued my postdoctoral research until 2023 at the FBK. Joining Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (BUAA) in November 2023, I am actively engaged in teaching and research at DTIRC, with a focus on digital manufacturing, digital engineering, design for sustainable additive manufacturing, and etc. I am passionate about mentoring students who are curious about research, hardworking, and dedicated to making meaningful contributions in these exciting fields.
